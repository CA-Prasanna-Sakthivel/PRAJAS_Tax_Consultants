ROC & MCA Filings

Scope of Work

  • Incorporation of companies and LLPs
  • Annual ROC compliance (AOC-4, MGT-7/7A, DIR-3 KYC)
  • Filing of resolutions (MGT-14, ADT-1, SH-7, PAS-3, DIR-12)
  • Director appointment, resignation, and DIN KYC
  • Compliance for increase in capital, conversion, closure, etc.

Key Benefits

  • Stay compliant with Companies Act & LLP Act
  • Avoid heavy penalties due to missed filings
  • Track & manage due dates effortlessly
  • Well-maintained statutory registers and records
  • Assistance in handling MCA notices or scrutiny

Our Process

  1. Review client structure and filing requirements
  2. Collate data and verify accuracy of documents
  3. Prepare and draft required forms/resolutions
  4. Submit filings via MCA portal
  5. Share acknowledgments and maintain compliance logs
