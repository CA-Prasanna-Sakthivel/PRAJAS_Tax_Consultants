Income Tax Filing & Planning

Scope of Work

We assist individuals, professionals, and businesses in preparing, reviewing, and filing their income tax returns. Our service includes classification of income sources, accurate computation of taxable income, claim of deductions/exemptions, and filing of returns with the Income Tax Department.

Key Benefits

  • Accurate & timely filing to avoid penalties
  • Maximize eligible deductions and tax savings
  • Personalized planning based on your profile
  • Expert handling of notices or queries
  • Compliant with the latest ITR forms and rules

Our Process

  1. Initial consultation and data collection
  2. Document review and clarification
  3. Preparation of draft return & discussion
  4. Finalization and online filing
  5. Form-Verification (ITR-V / e-Verify) assistance
