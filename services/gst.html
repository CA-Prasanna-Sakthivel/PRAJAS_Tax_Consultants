GST Registration & Filing

Scope of Work

  • GST registration for proprietors, partnerships, companies & LLPs
  • Monthly, quarterly, and annual GST return filings (GSTR-1, 3B, 9)
  • Reconciliation of GSTR-2A/2B with purchase register
  • Filing of Nil returns, amendments, and corrections
  • Advisory on GST applicability, ITC claims, and compliance strategy

Key Benefits

  • Full compliance with GST laws and notifications
  • Maximize eligible Input Tax Credit (ITC)
  • Avoid penalties due to missed or incorrect filings
  • Expert support for GST audits and queries
  • Automated reminders & timely updates

Our Process

  1. Initial registration or migration verification
  2. Data preparation and invoice classification
  3. Return preparation (GSTR-1, 3B, others)
  4. Review and finalize with the client
  5. File returns on GST portal with acknowledgment
